## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Kubescape">
    ```bash
    curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kubescape/kubescape/master/install.sh | /bin/bash
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first scan">
    ```bash
    kubescape scan
    ```
    Kubescape will scan your current cluster and display a security posture overview with compliance scores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore results and remediate">
    Drill into specific controls, scan individual workloads, or use `kubescape fix` to automatically remediate misconfigurations in your manifest files.
  </Step>
</Steps>
